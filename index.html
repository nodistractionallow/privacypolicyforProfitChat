Privacy Policy for Profit Chat

## Introduction

Welcome to Profit Chat. This Privacy Policy is meant to help you understand what information we collect, why we collect it, and how you can update, manage, export, and delete your information.

## Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The information Profit Chat collects, and how that information is used, depends on how you use our services. We may collect and store your chat messages, chat room codes, and unique IDs. We may also join your group chats or read your chats anonymously under certain circumstances.

## How We Use Information

We use the information we collect in existing services and for developing new ones. We'll ask for your consent before using your information for a purpose that isn't covered in this Privacy Policy.

## Your Privacy Controls

We want you to understand your rights and choices regarding how we use your information. Depending on your location, you may have certain rights under applicable data protection laws, including: - GDPR (if you're based in the EEA, Switzerland, or the UK) - CCPA (if you're based in California) - CalOPPA (if you're based in California)

## Points and Rewards

As a user of Profit Chat, you may earn points based on the time you spend on our app. These points can be redeemed for items such as recharges, shopping vouchers, etc. However, we retain the right to cancel the allocation of points, reject point redemption without any reason, and deduct charges and points. We may also impose fines in your currency for unfriendly activities.

## Government Access

In the event of a government request or national security threat, we prioritize government access over your privacy.

## Changes to This Policy

We change this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will not reduce your rights under this Privacy Policy without your explicit consent. We recommend you to review this policy regularly.
